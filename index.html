“ The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change. ”

― Carl R. Rogers

Welcome to the ‘Spirit in Body’ Practice. My aim is to make our work together collaborative and enjoyable. I invite women to meet their emotional challenges as opportunities for growth. I believe compassionate acceptance and understanding of one’s present situation paves the way for change. I fully support Roger’s humanistic view that people get hurt but are basically healthy. We all possess innate strengths waiting to be uncovered. My therapeutic approach supports you in understanding the relationship between your emotions, thoughts, physical experiences and spirituality. You will gain deeper insight, work through your issues, and develop helpful coping skills ranging from psychological tools, wellness practices and nutritional adjustments. Together we will tailor sessions to best suit your individual needs and wishes. By moving towards self-empowerment, my clients will discover a greater capacity for joy.

About Me

I am a US trained integral psychotherapist with a mind, body and spirit approach to healing. Drawing from psychodynamic, somatic, systemic and transpersonal approaches and the major spiritual traditions, I view psychological healing and growth within the larger context of spiritual unfolding. For the past 18 years, I have provided psychological coaching for diverse clients in the US and in Germany in both inpatient and outpatient settings. My nutritionist training has contributed to a more holistic approach and has further emphasized the key role of the body in our psychological well-being.

Fees and Practical Matters

‘Spirit in Body’ is a private practice. Coaching is offered in-person and online in both English and German. My fee is €120 for a 60-minute session. In some instances, private insurance companies may reimburse my services. My practice rooms are located in downtown Berlin.

Due to the COVID pandemic, all my sessions are currently conducted online.
